Amar Desai

PhD Assistant Professor Case Comprehensive Cancer Center · CWRU

Amar is an Assistant Professor at the Case Comprehensive Cancer Center at Case Western Reserve University. He received his undergraduate degree in Molecular Biology from Kenyon College and his PhD in Pharmacology from Case Western Reserve University. His research focuses on understanding how hematopoietic stem cells regenerate across aging, injury, and disease, with the goal of developing better treatments for blood disorders and improving bone marrow transplantation outcomes.

Amar is a devout Cleveland sports fan and believes a lifetime of sports despair has prepared him well for a career in science.
